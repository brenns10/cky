Detailed Description

A struct to represent a rule in a CNF CFG.

CNF grammars are context-free grammars that are restricted in their form. The rules may have the form `A -> b`, where `b` is a terminal symbol, or they may be of the form `A -> B C`, where `B` and `C` are non-terminal symbols.
