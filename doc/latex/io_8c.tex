\-This function reads a transition from a line. \-The transition can have an arbitrary number of ranges, each separated by a space. \-The line should be of the form\-: `\-X-\/\-Y\-:\mbox{[}+$|$-\/\mbox{]}\-A-\/\-B\mbox{[}\mbox{[}\-A-\/\-B\mbox{]}...\mbox{]}`, where `\-X` and `\-Y` are state numbers, `\-A` and `\-B` are characters forming a range, and the `+` or `-\/` determine whether the transition is positive (only those in the range) or negative (everything not in that range). \-Note that there is only one `+` or `-\/`; it applies for all ranges in the transition.
